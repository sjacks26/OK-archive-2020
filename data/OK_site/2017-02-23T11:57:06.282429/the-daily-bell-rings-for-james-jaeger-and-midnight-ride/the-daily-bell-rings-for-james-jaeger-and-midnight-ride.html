James Jaeger Fast At Work

 

James Jaeger Introduces Oath Keepers’ “Ten Orders” To

Readers At The Daily Bell

 

The Daily Bell has posted an extensive interview with James Jaeger of Matrix Entertainment. In this interview James allows Anthony Wile to direct the flow of topics to be covered and the interview is an excellent read. But what is of chief interest to us here at Oath Keepers is the dandy plug James gave our “Ten Orders We Will Not Obey”.

James also talks about independent film making and of course spends some time promoting his current project, MIDNIGHT RIDE, the twin film of his previous movie about the Second Amendment, MOLON LABE.  Oath Keepers has been working with James Jaeger for several years because he produces the highest quality documentaries on Constitutional topics. That sort of film-making goes hand in hand with the Oath Keepers missions of RT&I and CPT.

Oath Keepers’ membership supported MOLON LABE very admirably, and our members are continuing to send in their support for MIDNIGHT RIDE.  In turn, James Jaeger promotes Oath Keepers more powerfully than any other man I know in the liberty movement. Additionally, James works closely with wonderful freedom fighters such as Ron Paul, G. Edward Griffin, Edwin Vieira, Stewart Rhodes and many others. It is a pleasure to work in tandem with James’ Matrix Entertainment studios, and the relationship is a “win-win” for all concerned, including the fate of America’s Constitutionalist movement. But while expressing my sincere gratitude to our members for your continuing support, let’s get back to James’ interview with Anthony Wile at The Daily Bell.

The interview ranges from Hollywood mass-antics to the nature of government; from independent film making to the global economy; from the welfare-warfare state to the power elite; from Tesla technology to Oath Keepers — with lots more packed into the interview. As your editor, I’m strongly recommending this lengthy interview as a “must read”. Please give yourself permission to enjoy this insightful look into the mind of the independent film maker who so strongly believes in the Oath Keepers mission, and, after reading it yourself, pass it around to friends and family. The diversity of thought, the depth of inspection of all things in today’s world, the analytical synthesis with which James accumulates both the subtle and candid aspects of our lives in the post-modern world, can only benefit the reader.

Oath Keepers salutes James Jaeger of Matrix Entertainment and Anthony Wile of The Daily Bell.
