Testimonial – Calvin Russell

0 0 0 0
February 5, 2015

Testimonial: I am proud to find like minded people that will always honor the oath that they took, that I took. Not on my watch is not just for the 6 years that I served in the NAVY from 31OCT01 til 31OCT07. My watch will continue to be stood until death. I love my country and I am proud to call myself an American.

Unfortunately, we are always on watch due to the politics and ethics of those who call themselves friend or foe. When I first took the oath and got sworn into the NAVY I really didn’t understand what it meant. I really didn’t understand what it meant until I got out of the Navy and started waking up to all that is going on in our country. I am just glad that I did wake up. A friend of mine told me about this website and I had to join instantly. It’s great to see like minded people here.
