Testimonial – Zackery Mock

0 0 0 0
February 24, 2015

Testimonial: I enlisted in the US Army Reserves at 17, as a junior in high school. I completed Basic Training before reaching my senior year. Before graduating, I was involved in a serious car accident that left me in a permanent ‘non-deploy’ status. I spent the remainder of my time in the military teaching ‘scout’ soldiers, a program designed to prepare new enlistees for their career in the military.

I swore to uphold and protect the ideals set forth by my fore fathers in the US Constitution, and I made sure to instill that philosophy in my students.  Though I may not have been given the chance to serve my country in a combat situation, I feel that my contributions to the armed forces live on in the form of the morals I taught to the new generation.

To those who served, thank you.
To those who died, I will never forget.
To those who who keep the Oath, I love you.
