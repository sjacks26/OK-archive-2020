by Elias Alias , April 26, 2015

Film makers traditionally consider a successfully-filmed segment of a movie-in-progress to be “In The Can”, as reels of exposed film were to be kept in round cans, right? James Jaeger is a film producer who has many years experience making films, and has a habit of talking in movie-making jargon. So when James told me that Ron Paul was “in the can”, I knew what he meant, and now you do too. AWESOME!

 

Ron Paul is now in the movie

MIDNIGHT RIDE!

James Jaeger tells me that Ron Paul emailed to James a “success” message expressing his satisfaction with the interview, and James has now reviewed the footage in his Pennsylvania studio. He tells me that Dr. Paul is looking great throughout his interview for the movie, so all of us who have been working on getting that movie done are thrilled.

But it gets even better! In the past week and a half we also have interviewed David R. Gillie and Jack Rooney, both of whom appeared in the Second Amendment film MOLON LABE, also by James Jaeger.

 

And to catch the interest of the Tea Party masses we’re proud to announce that –
