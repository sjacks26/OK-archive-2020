Testimonial – John Young

0 0 0 0
February 11, 2015

Testimonial: My family and I came to America from England in April 1954 . We went thru all of the then necessary requirements to immigrate . While I was in 8th grade we became American citizens , that was the first time I swore my oath to America . May 1966 I joined the US Navy , I swore my allegiance to America a second time in the same federal courthouse as my first . I ended up in the Seabees and volunteered all the way thru for whatever I could get ending in ‘Nam , thus spending ’68-’69 and first days of ’70 in country . I’ve told folks that my volunteering for enlistment in the US military was my way of saying “thank you for allowing me to come to your country”.
