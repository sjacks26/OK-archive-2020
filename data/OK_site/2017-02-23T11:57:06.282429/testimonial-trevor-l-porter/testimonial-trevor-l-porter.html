Testimonial – Trevor L Porter

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , January 22, 2015

Testimonial: First of all I would like to thank retired and active soldiers in our
armed forces. I have never served in any branch of the military, but I am a very proud American and Statesman, and support all of our military branches. Over the past few years I have watched as our Constitution has been ignored and abused by those in some of the highest places in our own Capitals and Senate. I never looked, or thought, about joining a militia, but now I feel I need to do whatever I can now to help protect and preserve our rights as Americans. I know I want my children to be protected and have  peace of mind in this great country of ours. I am very proud to call myself an Oath Keeper and American.

Thank You
Trevor L Porter
