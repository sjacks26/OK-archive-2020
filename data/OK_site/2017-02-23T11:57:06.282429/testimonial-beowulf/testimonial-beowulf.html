Testimonial – Beowulf

January 22, 2015

Testimonial: Hello Brothers,
My name is James York (Beowulf) and I joined Oath Keepers when a friend of mine told me about the site and what the members stand for. I served with pride in the U.S. Army for 8 years before becoming a Deputy Sheriff to help serve the county in which I live. While I believe there is no greater service a man can do than to serve his country and where he lives I believe that we must protect the rights of the American people as well, doing this on a daily basis in my job…

I took an oath to Uphold and Defend the CONSTITUTION OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA AGAINST ALL ENEMIES FOREIGN AND DOMESTIC. I try to live that oath daily in my life, I will not stand idly by and let the 2nd Amendment be walked on. I believe our forefathers fought for our right to keep and bear arms and it is my duty as a Vet and Deputy Sheriff to uphold these rights.

Beo,
