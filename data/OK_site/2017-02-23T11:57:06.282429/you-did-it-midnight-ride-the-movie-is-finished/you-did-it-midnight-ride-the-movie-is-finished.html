You Did It! Midnight Ride the movie is Finished!

 

Good News for Oath Keepers!

Yes, I’m excited and I want to salute our Oath Keepers membership for making this film possible.  During the past 10 months I have made a number of appeals for donations/contributions by posting articles here at national and emailing those articles out to our full membership list and our support lists. Our members and friends contributed grandly to the production of the movie. The movie, as James Jaeger likes to say, is “in the can”. (That’s an old film-makers’ way of saying that the film is completed.) And all of you did it. THANK YOU!

Oath Keepers is represented nicely in this movie, which features Stewart Rhodes, Chuck Baldwin, Sheriff Mack, former Board member David R. Gillie, and your humble (but grateful!) servant. And Oath Keepers is looking good in the opening credits as well as in the end credits.

James has asked me to extend his gratitude to each of you.  I am very proud of you. Many of you will be in the end credits for the movie, so get ready to see your names on the screen as of November 06, 2015.

You will be able to view the film for free if you’re available on the Sixth of November for a brief several hours of time on YouTube, just the way we did with this movie’s sister film, MOLON LABE. On November 06 2015 I will post a “Premiere Page” here at Oath Keepers national, and at the time James has announced below you will be able to screen the movie for free right here. The Premiere Page will be in our front page “slider window”, and as James and I count down the minutes, I’ll take the movie “active” right here for you and your friends. James’ update follows as the letter he sent for me to share with you all:

–

Dear MIDNIGHT RIDE Associate,

 

I am pleased to inform you that MIDNIGHT RIDE – When Rogue Politicians Call for Martial Law is now complete. This is our 7th political film on the U.S. Constitution, the previous ones are on YouTube  as a public service.

We will have an Internet Premiere for MIDNIGHT RIDE on Friday, 06 November 2015 from 8PM – 1AM midnight Eastern time (5PM – 10PM Pacific). If you want to be one of the websites that hosts the Premiere, please let me know as soon as possible.

Between now and then, if you would like to pre-order DVDs of MIDNIGHT RIDE, please go to http://www.MidnightRide.us/dvd Pre-orders will be shipped on the Premiere date, however we cannot guarantee when you will actually receive them. If we run out of DVDs, pre-orders will be fulfilled in first priority.

If, after watching the MIDNIGHT RIDE premiere, you would like to get into the screencredits, we will still have the door open for donations. Several Associate Producer and one Executive Producer screencredits are still available in the main credits of the movie. These donations will be used to help market the movie to wider audiences. To donate, go to http://www.MidnightRide.us/donate and see what credits are still available in the main and tail credits.

Thank you for helping us make this movie. We hope you enjoy it and feel it was a worthwhile endeavor. If you do, we hope you will tell family, friends and associates about the movie and/or send them a DVD.
A special thanks goes to my main producing partners, EDWIN VIEIRA, ELIAS ALIAS, RICHARD IOTT, BRIAN ROCKEY and CAROL SNYDER. You are all fantastic and it was a pleasure working with you.

Also, I want to thank OATH KEEPERS, BRAEBURN ENTERTAINMENT, the OK Membership and GUN OWNERS OF AMERICA for stepping up and helping make this important film inspired by EDWIN VIEIRA’s latest book — BY TYRANNY OUT OF NECESSITY: The Bastardy of Martial Law — happen. Thanks Edwin for researching and writing another vital work.

Given the subject matter of MIDNIGHT RIDE, you can be assured this film will NOT appear in the MainStream Media. We go into the reasons why in the movie. Thus, if you want to see MIDNIGHT RIDE widely distributed, please either contribute to the marketing budget or help market the movie yourself.

You can help market the movie by having a Premiere in your local area. Such Premiere can be in your living room, at your local library or movie theater, or on your local public access TV channel. Just let me know and I will get you a master of the show in time for your premiere.

Again, thank you for your belief in the project and your financial and spiritual support. Our research, and these films, proceed on the backs of concerned men and women who know we have the greatest country in history, but who seek to build an even “more perfect union”.

Sincerely,

JAMES JAEGER
Producer/Director

Again let me thank all of you for your generosity and support.  It is my opinion that this film will be one of the finest tools for RT&I in Oath Keepers’ six-year history, and will by far be the best promotional film for our organization done to date.

Salute!

Elias Alias, editor
