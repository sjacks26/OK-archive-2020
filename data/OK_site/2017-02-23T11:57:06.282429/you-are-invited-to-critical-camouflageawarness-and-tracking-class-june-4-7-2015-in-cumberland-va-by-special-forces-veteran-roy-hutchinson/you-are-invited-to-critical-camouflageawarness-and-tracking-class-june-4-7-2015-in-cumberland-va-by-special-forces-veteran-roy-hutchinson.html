NOTE FROM STEWART: Tracking and awareness are critical skills. So is camouflage. You must be aware of your surroundings and who and what is active near you and in your area. Tracking vastly expands your capability to know where the enemy is (whether that enemy is in war or in “normal” times) and tracking helps you find lost people, lost animals, and game. It is also a critical military skill that is now experiencing a revival, such as in the Marine Corps’ Combat Hunter program. And camouflage is also vital. In combat, if you can be seen, you can be shot. Roy is an excellent teacher, and you will leave his class with a greatly improved capability. I look forward to seeing you there if you can make it. – Stewart Rhodes
