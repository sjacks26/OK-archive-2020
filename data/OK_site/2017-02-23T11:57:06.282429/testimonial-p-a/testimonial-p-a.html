Testimonial: I am a 71 year old gal who rides a motorcycle. I worked on the Nuclear tests, I worked in the medical field for 39 yrs. and I want to stay free. I want the America that was here when I was a teenager. That was when God was still a part of our school life as well as our lives. He is still all of mine. I vow with all of you who love our country and what it was, never to give in to tyranny. I will never give them my guns or my life. My soul belongs to God, and my guns belong only to me. I stand with you in libery and justice! COME AND GET ME!
