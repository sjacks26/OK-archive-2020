Rick Sowell:

Testimonial: I grew up in a very patriotic extended family in the 60’s and 70’s in East Texas. Most of the men in my family had served in WW1, WW2, Korea or Vietnam.  Many members of the family would gather on Sunday afternoons and discuss current affairs, politics and the weather as many were ranchers or farmers. Invariably the conversation would turn to the current political situation (the Vietnam War) and the political climate of the country. The overwhelming consensus was that America was established as a Christian nation with our God given rights enumerated in the constitution and that communism/socialism was a threat to that ideal. The ideal was that Americans opposed to communism were carrying the torch that was lit by our forefathers.

My family was involved in settling and founding this nation and that was something that was not lost on the generation that lived during the great war and the depression. The principles of freedom and our bill of rights was most certainly of paramount importance to these great men. I was told directly that the agents of socialism were actively attempting to undermine our nation by attacking the core principles that our nation was founded upon. I knew that my task was to pick up the torch and carry it as long as I was able.

I joined the military during my junior year in High School and took the oath to uphold and defend the constitution for the rest of my life. I served in the 82nd Airborne Division and the 4th ID as an Infantryman, was separated from service in 1991 due to a service connected disability incurred in the line of duty. I had planned to serve and retire from the military, not as a job, but out of a sense of duty.

It is obvious to me that the agents of socialism are rapidly gaining ground through manipulation of the social consciousness via the media and our educational system. The degradation of patriotism and our rights during the last 15-20 years has generated great concern on my part. I have been and am becoming more concerned of late with respect to the direction that our country is heading. I hereby reaffirm my oath and stand ready to do whatever is necessary to uphold our constitution and defend America against all enemies foreign and domestic. I believe in the constitution as it was written and the rule of law. I support our law enforcement community and our patriotic citizens but believe that forces are actively working to destroy our nation as it was intended by our founders. I am thankful that Stewart founded Oath Keepers as an organization intended to defend our constitution and therefore America. As Oath Keepers, we must make whatever sacrifices are necessary to protect this great nation.

Rick

Joseph Forrest:

Testimonial:  I joined the service in April 2001. Of course you know what happened a few short months later. The terrorist acts of 9/11 were terrible. The way that our leadership has used these acts to suppress liberty and justice is completely intolerable. I said an oath to support and defend our Constitution against enemies both foreign and domestic. Today, we have more enemies than we can imagine and every one of them seem to be sitting in positions of power in Washington, D.C.

As a man who took an oath to defend the Constitution, I swear on my life to uphold the following:

I will NEVER obey an order to disarm an American citizen.

I will NEVER support or conduct warrant-less searches.

I will NEVER obey an order to detain American citizens as “unlawful enemy combatants” or to subject them to trial by military tribunal.

I will NEVER obey orders to impose martial law or a “state of emergency” on a state, or to enter with force into a state, without the express consent and invitation of that state’s legislature and governor.

I will NEVER obey orders to invade and subjugate any state that asserts its sovereignty and declares the national government to be in violation of the compact by which that state entered the Union.

I will NEVER obey any order to blockade American cities.

I will NEVER obey any order to force American citizens into any form of detention camps under any pretext.

I will NEVER obey orders to assist or support the use of any foreign troops on U.S. soil against the American people to “keep the peace” or to “maintain control” during any emergency, or under any other pretext. We will consider such use of foreign troops against our people to be an invasion and an act of war.

I will NEVER obey any orders to confiscate the property of the American people, including food and other essential supplies, under any emergency pretext whatsoever.

I will NEVER obey any orders which infringe on the right of the people to free speech, to peaceably assemble, and to petition their government for a redress of grievances.

So help me God.
