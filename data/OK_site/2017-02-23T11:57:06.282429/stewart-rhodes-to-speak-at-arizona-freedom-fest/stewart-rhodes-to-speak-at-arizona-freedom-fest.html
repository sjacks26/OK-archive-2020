Stewart Rhodes To Speak At Arizona Freedom Fest

0 0 0 0
April 18, 2015

 

Stewart Rhodes and Sheriff Mack will be speaking at the Arizona Freedom Fest, May 1-3, at Shots Ranch, in Kingman, Arizona. Joel Skousen and Ammon Bundy will speak also. Jordan Page will play his Liberty songs.
