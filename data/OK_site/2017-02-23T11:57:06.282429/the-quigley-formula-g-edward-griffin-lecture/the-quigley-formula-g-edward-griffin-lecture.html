The Quigley Formula – G. Edward Griffin Lecture

0 0 0 0
November 5, 2015

Here is a terrific lecture from G. Edward Griffin, about the Rhodes Round Table, and Carroll Quigley, who wrote about it, as an insider, and praised it. Quigley was a professor at Georgetown University, and was the mentor of Bill Clinton. If you are unfamiliar with the Rhodes Round Table, the Council on Foreign Relations (CFR) or Carroll Quigley, you will find this an extremely good primer. Near the end, he lists many past and current members of the CFR, which will surprise you.
