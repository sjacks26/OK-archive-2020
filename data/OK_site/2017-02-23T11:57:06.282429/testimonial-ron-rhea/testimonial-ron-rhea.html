Testimonial: I took an oath when I entered the United States military to defend the constitution and that was against ALL enemies of it, whether it be a pretender in high places or a left-wing, anti-rights moron, or a radical invader on the sovereignty of this nation, whether they crossed the border illegally or hijack airplanes in the name of their god. If I die in service to my God and my country, I will be in good company of the millions who have done it before.
