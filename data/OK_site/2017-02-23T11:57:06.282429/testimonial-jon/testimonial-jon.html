0 0 0 0
February 20, 2015

Testimonial: I served a total of 29 years and 5 months in the US Air Force. I never really thought too much about the oath of enlistment until I reached 10 years of service. After the first Gulf war I realized what the oath really was all about. Since I retired in 2010 I have watched, helplessly, as our government has systematically destroyed our country and watched in horror as the current “liar in chief” has made a mockery of the United States and the Constitution. I am with Oath Keepers, to the end.
