by Larry Diffey , November 16, 2014

Testimonial: I joined the Army in 1965 and Attended basic training at Ft. Jackson, SC, and went through AIT at Aberdeen Proving Grounds MD. I was stationed at Assachuffenburg, Germany in 1965, and 1966. I volunteered for Vietnam, and was stationed at Bien Hoa Mac v base from 1967 through 1968. I re-enlisted and stayed in Vietnam at Can Tho Air base, in the Mecong Delta till I left in 1969.

I have been shooting since I was 10 years old, and still love it today. I own 7 rifles, and 3 handguns. I own a 50 acre camp in the woods of Maine, and shoot there often. No one else is near me there, and I have plenty of water, wild game, and berries. I bought this property because of it’s isolation, and is easy to defend.
