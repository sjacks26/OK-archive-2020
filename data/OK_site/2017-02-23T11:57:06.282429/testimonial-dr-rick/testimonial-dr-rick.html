Testimonial – Dr. Rick

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , January 26, 2015

Testimonial: The US Constitution never states a weapon type or power. It simply states “The right to bear arms.” If we begin to differentiate between weapons we weaken the document. The US Constitution is applied equally or not at all. You do NOT choose the parts that you like.

The founders of the US Constitution fought a bloody battle against the British to create this document and our Country. History will show the reasons for this document.

It is important for all US Citizens to read their Constitution and understand the
freedoms it has given them.

God Bless America and all it’s Citizens!!
