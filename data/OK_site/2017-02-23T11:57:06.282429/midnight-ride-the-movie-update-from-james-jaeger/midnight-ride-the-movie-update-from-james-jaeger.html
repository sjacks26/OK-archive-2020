MIDNIGHT RIDE, The Movie: Update From James Jaeger

0 0 0 0
by Elias Alias , February 10, 2015

James Jaeger Concentrates On His Work

A note from your friendly Oath Keepers editor:

James Jaeger periodically sends email updates on the progress of his current project, which presently is the remarkable movie documentary about the four types of Martial Law and the view which true scholarship of the Constitution reveals about Martial Law.  The film will also take a close look at the militarization of America’s police. The movie MIDNIGHT RIDE is more than that — it is also a very classy showcase for the Oath Keepers mission, to the promotion of our “Ten Orders We Will Not Obey”.  Yes, Oath Keepers is helping James Jaeger fund the production of this important film, and the film will feature Oath Keepers in many ways.

For all of those reasons and more, I am working very closely with James Jaeger and with Oath Keepers’ membership to help in any way I can.  I truly want to see this movie completed and sent out into today’s very crazy world, for the American people will need the information this film will give the public.  This is a very good way to do something powerful for Liberty and Freedom, and an excellent way to get the Oath Keepers mission “on the record” with a movie featuring Ron Paul, G. Edward Griffin, Edwin Vieira, Stewart Rhodes, Sheriff Mack, Chuck Baldwin, and a number of other Constitutionalists who are recognized in their respective fields. To do this sort of film, James works tirelessly to coordinate so many people for their filmed interviews, the camera crews, make-up artists, location facility owners/managers, tech people, studio people, etc etc.

I am amazed at the work that goes into making a documentary of this caliber, so I am honored to be a part of this. And so should every Oath Keeper member and/or supporter who has sent in money to help fund this project. Last month, January 2015, Oath Keepers members raised more than five thousand dollars to finance the four interviews listed below in James’ update. James and I cannot thank you enough, and we both hope each of you who has contributed to the film has a warm glow inside your heart of hearts, a glow which comes from knowing you personally helped do something very powerful for Liberty, for the Constitution.

So please accept our sincere Thank You!, from James Jaeger, myself, and the leadership of Oath Keepers national. Well done! January’s interviews are completed now, thanks to you, and February’s interviews, starting with Ron Paul himself, are being scheduled as we speak. Coming up also are interviews with Sheriff Mack and KrisAnne Hall. I am excited and can’t wait to see footage of those three interviews.

To those reading this who have yet to send in a contribution, please know that James Jaeger is an independent film-maker and all of his Constitution-themed documentaries have been paid for by the public. After he covers the costs, James places the movies online for the public to view for free, as his contribution to the American Constitutionalist movement. It works this way — as the money comes in to his studio, he schedules more interviews. Once all the interviews are completed and filmed, he takes the work for a couple of months or so to his high-tech studios in Pennsylvania and puts everything all together with his best expertise.  The timing for release of each new film has been determined by the rapidity in which people in the grass-roots public donated their dollars toward production. Because of the pending militarized police state staring Americans in the face right now, we think that this movie needs to be completed and launched into the public sooner than later.

So how about it — can you, will you, take a moment and send your donations to James Jaeger?  You can join the others who have already done so, in knowing that you did what you could to place Stewart Rhodes, our Founder and President, and Chuck Baldwin, our national Chaplain, and Sheriff Mack, member of Oath Keepers’ Board of Directors, all into one power-packed documentary featuring Ron Paul, and all speaking to address a script inspired by the genius of Dr. Edwin Vieira, Jr., who’s book, “By Tyranny Out Of Necessity: The Bastardy Of Martial Law” is to be promoted strongly by this movie — as it should be!  So bear all this in mind as you read James’ latest update, below, and decide in your heart and mind that your dollars will indeed help this movie get done by this Spring or early Summer (depending on how fast the donations come in). You’ll be proud of yourself, and rightly so. Thank you for your help. A lot of people will appreciate it.

Here is James Jaeger’s current update. Enjoy. And thanks again for your support.

Salute!

Elias Alias, editor for Oath Keepers, associate producer for MIDNIGHT RIDE
