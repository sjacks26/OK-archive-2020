France’s Hollande Proposes Creation Of Euro-Zone Government

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , July 22, 2015

By Shorty Dawkins

Bloomberg News reports that French President Francois Hollande, in an opinion piece, is calling for the creation of a Euro-Zone Government.

“Circumstances are leading us to accelerate,” Hollande said in an opinion piece published by the Journal du Dimanche on Sunday. “What threatens us is not too much Europe, but a lack of it.”

Oh my! What a surprise! The President of France is calling for a Euro-Zone government? Unbelievable!

Actually, it should come as no surprise, as it has been the plan of the globalist, one-world-government leadership to first unite Europe under one government, then other nations in other regions, and finally a single world government.

What I find interesting is the first part of the quote above:  “Circumstances are leading us to accelerate,”. He is referring, it is to be assumed, to the “Greek Crisis”, along with the indebtedness of other European nations, such as Portugal, Spain, Italy, Ireland, and even France, itself. The indebtedness that was manufactured by the globalists themselves.  It is a classic example of the Hegelian dialectic: create a problem, wait for the reaction from the public (solve the problem, please), then have the predetermined solution prepared. It’s a con-game, really, and the “marks” are the unsuspecting people.

“What threatens us is not too much Europe, but a lack of it.” This has been the endgame since before the Common Market ever came into existence. It is precisely why the Common Market was started, followed by the expanded European Common Market, complete with its own Parliament, including new expanded powers. Now the call is renewed for a Euro-Zone government. Others have called for it in the past, of course, but the new call is a clarion call, for “Circumstances are leading us to accelerate”.

My question is: Could those “circumstances” actually be that the “marks” are starting to catch on to the con? The blatant obviousness of the push for a One World Government, by the True-Believers, hints that they are in a hurry. They no longer hide their actions. As the old saying goes: Something evil this way comes.

Buckle your seat belts, we are in for a bumpy ride.
