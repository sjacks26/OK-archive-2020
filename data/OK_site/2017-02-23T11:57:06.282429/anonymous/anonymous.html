Anonymous – Active Duty Army Medic

0 0 0 0
by Larry Diffey , November 8, 2014

Testimonial: Active Duty Army, SGT, Tour in Afghanistan, Medic.

I remember my oath, I have taken it three times, and long after I leave active duty, I will remain active.

I take my oath seriously!  Always have and always will, no matter where I find myself.  If I didn’t mean it, I wouldn’t have said it. My family has military in our blood.

I wish more people really stopped to think about the oath they took and whether or not they really meant it or if they even mean it now. I will do my best to never let my comrades forget not only the oath we share, but more importantly, it’s meaning!
