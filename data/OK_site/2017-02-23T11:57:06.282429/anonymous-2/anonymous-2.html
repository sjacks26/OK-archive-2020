Testimonial:  As a seventeen year old kid, I stood with a hundred or so new recruits and took and oath before God that I would protect my country. I ended that oath with these words:
“So Help Me God”

I did then, and I do now, take that very serious. I didn’t make a promise to God, I swore an oath to him. And now, in the December of my life, I fear that only some of my generation are left to protect my country again. I know we have some fine people in the military now who feel the same way but, I feel they are few. They are few just as those of us who are left are few.
We still believe in; “Not On My Watch”
