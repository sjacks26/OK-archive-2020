The following video gives a basic overview of the goals of Agenda 21. It is called Agenda 21 For Dummies, which grates on me, because one goal of Agenda 21 is the dumbing down of students. I prefer to refer to it as a primer on Agenda 21. Make no mistake, Agenda 21 is the UN process for seizing total control of everyone on the planet, in every aspect of your life: jobs, housing, food, education, the environment, and restrictions on all your liberties.
