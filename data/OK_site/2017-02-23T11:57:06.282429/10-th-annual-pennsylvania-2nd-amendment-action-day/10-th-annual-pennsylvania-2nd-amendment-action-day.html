10 th Annual Pennsylvania 2nd Amendment Action Day

0 0 0 0
May 14, 2015

by Andy Pannafino

Hundreds turned out for the 10th Annual Second Amendment Rally in downtown Harrisburg. Lawmakers and Second Amendment supporters gave speeches and mingled with fellow gun rights advocates on the Pennsylvania Capitol steps.

A strong group of PA Oath Keepers showed up at the event. A few shown in picture above.

Col. Joe Junguzza delivered a most powerful speech .

Sheriff Richard Mack was the keynote speaker and impressed everyone in attendance.

Our good friend Larry Pratt from Gun Owners of America helped kickoff the event.

PA State Rep. Daryl Metcalfe hosted the event.
