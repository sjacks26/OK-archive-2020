by Larry Diffey , November 20, 2014

Testimonial:  Since I was a boy, I was taught to love my country. My beloved nation, that gives freedom and opportunity to all of her citizens. I swore my oath to support and defend the Constitution of the United States, so help me God.

I’m saddened by the gradual decline in our nation,  our government, and our leaders. I serve no man in office, no political party. I serve the people of the United States, who are protected by their Rights in the Constitution. I will willingly lay down my life to see those rights are protected from any enemy foreign AND domestic.

I have 1 tour in Afghanistan and cherish my service to my country. I cherish those brothers and sisters whom I served with. I joined the military because of my love of country and duty to give back. God Bless America.
