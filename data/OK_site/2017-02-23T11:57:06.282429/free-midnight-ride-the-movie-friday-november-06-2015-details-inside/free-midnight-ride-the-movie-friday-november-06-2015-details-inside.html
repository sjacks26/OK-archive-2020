In our article of October 19 2015 we announced the completion of James Jaeger’s latest Constitution-related documentary movie,

MIDNIGHT RIDE: When Rogue Politicians Call For Martial Law.

Now you are invited to watch the movie for free, right here at Oath Keepers national, and then help us raise money for promoting the film outward to the American public by purchasing your personal copy and helping us spread the word. When you buy your copy of the movie here at the Oath Keepers store you will be helping James Jaeger and his Matrix Entertainment studios as well as adding your support to the Oath Keepers mission.

Oath Keepers is heavily represented in this film, and proudly helped do the fund-raising which made the movie possible. Our membership raised many thousands of dollars toward production. Stewart Rhodes, Founder of Oath Keepers, is joined in the film by our national chaplain Chuck Baldwin, Board member Sheriff Mack, former Board members David R. Gillie and myself, Elias Alias (your friendly editor).  Also in the film are: Larry Pratt of Gun Owners of America (GOA); former Congressman Ron Paul; author/analyst Pat Buchanan; former Cty Commissioner (Madison, MT) Dan Happel; author/lecturer/Constitutional Attorney Edwin Vieira; Tea Party co-founder Walter Reddy; activist Rosie Haas; and author G. Edward Griffin (The Creature From Jekyll Island). The cast is completed with Eric Pratt, Sheila Matthews, and Jack Rooney.

This film looks at the *three* militia clauses in the U.S. Constitution as well as the Second Amendment in our Bill of Rights while offering scholarly insights into the intent of the Constitution regarding “Martial Law”.  The movie also features Oath Keepers’ “Ten Orders We Shall Not Obey”, and is packed with the Oath Keepers philosophy. The film was inspired by Dr. Edwin Vieira, Jr.’s, latest book –

BY TYRANNY OUT OF NECESSITY: The Bastardy of Martial Law

In thanking all our contributors and supporters, James and I are happy to announce the premiere for Friday, November 06 2015. Here is the time frame in which all readers here are invited to screen the movie for free –

 

Friday, November 06 2015

8pm to 1 a.m. midnight Eastern time

7:00 pm to Midnight Central time

6:00 pm to 11:00 pm Mountain time

5pm to 10pm Pacific time

 

NOTE: The movie is 111 minutes in length.

 

To watch the movie, simply re-open this article at the time listed above. I will place the video into this article just minutes before it  “goes live”. I will take the movie down at the time designated above, so please note the time carefully — you have a five hour window for viewing the film here.

Enjoy!
