Testimonial – John H Roca

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , January 22, 2015

Testimonial:  I had taken my oath in July of 1978 to uphold and defend the
Constitution of the UNITED STATES OF AMERICA ! Against all enemies foreign and domestic, and I PLEDGE my LIFE for the love of my GOD, MY COUNTRY, Family and friends SO HELP ME GOD ! ! WE ARE ONE NATION UNDER GOD! ! ! ! ! To the powers that be, who say our constitution is just a peice of paper, and those who wish to destroy our Nation I will say to you this. GOOD will always triumph over evil. God is watching and your wicked ways will surely be your demise. Eternity is forever ! ! ! !

MAY THE GOOD LORD WATCH OVER ALL WHO MAY BE CALLED UPON AGAIN TO COME TO THE DEFENSE OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA ! ! ! IN GOD I DO TRUST !

John H Roca
Veteran U S A F.
