]]> By: mark https://www.oathkeepers.org/what-is-your-ultimate-default-plan/#comment-60037 Tue, 29 Dec 2015 14:09:42 +0000 https://www.oathkeepers.org/?p=9198#comment-60037 Sitting down, envisioning and writing down a plan to fall back one that is quite close to subsistence is a good idea. The problem I see when I talk to people who have done this is that they have fallen into the same trap as any ideological people will. It looks good on paper but will it actually work to keep you alive? Write up your plan then actually go see if you can survive camping in cold weather or planting a garden where you decide to stay (you may be surprised that your favorite vegetable won’t grow well or that you should have taken the family cat to keep vermin off it). Simple things like finding a spot to camp that is bug free, doesn’t flood during heavy rains or is protected from storms is not easy to do. Falling off the learning curve (and you will) while you still have a safety net is okay. Falling off the learning curve in a survival situation will get you killed. ]]>
