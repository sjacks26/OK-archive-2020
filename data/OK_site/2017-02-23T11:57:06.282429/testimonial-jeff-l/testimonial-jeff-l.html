Testimonial – Jeff L.

January 23, 2015

Testimonial: My enlistment in the United States Army was originally to get away from my Mothers second husband. A strange thing happened on March 31, 1972, I graduated from Basic Training at Fort Leonardwood, Missouri. It was that day of my life that became the most important and joyful day “I became a Soldiering the United States Army and realized that it was my honor and obligation to defend my Country. I spent almost 10 years in the United States Army and would not trade that time for anything. My Oath of Allegiance did not end the day I left the service. I continually watch and defend this country with my vote and if needed will let my Life and Blood cleanse her of any that wish to attack it.
