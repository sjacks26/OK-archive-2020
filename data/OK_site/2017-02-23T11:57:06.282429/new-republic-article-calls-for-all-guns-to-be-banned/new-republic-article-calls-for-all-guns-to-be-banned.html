New Republic Article Calls For All Guns To Be Banned

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , December 11, 2015

 

Phoebe Maltz Bovy, in her article, “It’s Time to Ban Guns. Yes, All of Them.”, in the New Republic, says:

Ban guns. All guns. Get rid of guns in homes, and on the streets, and, as much as possible, on police. Not just because of San Bernardino, or whichever mass shooting may pop up next, but also not not because of those. Don’t sort the population into those who might do something evil or foolish or self-destructive with a gun and those who surely will not. As if this could be known—as if it could be assessed without massively violating civil liberties and stigmatizing the mentally ill. Ban guns! Not just gun violence. Not just certain guns. Not just already-technically-illegal guns. All of them.

Gun-control was never about controlling some guns, though countless gun-grabbers claimed it was. It has always been about banning guns, one incremental step at a time. The gun-grabbers want us disarmed, so they can control us. They want us subservient to their Utopian New World Order, a neo-feudalistic, collectivist dream of theirs, in which the world, and everyone in it, is controlled by a few “benevolent intellects” who know what is best for us; a world of zombies, endlessly wandering through a dull world, working in our allotted spaces, so the Lords and Masters may live in grandeur, because they are better than the rest of us.

Ms. Bovy, not surprisingly, never says who will take our guns away. (Hint: It won’t be her.) She could care less if there is a revolution, in which millions could die. She wants guns banned, not to save lives, but to make us subservient. Any fool knows that criminals and terrorists will get guns. They could care less what the law says. Criminals, terrorists and tyrants always want the populace disarmed. Hitler did, Stalin did, and Mao did. Once disarmed, they slaughtered their own people.

Never bring a knife to a gunfight, the old saying goes. If the gun-grabbers have their way, that will be the only option left for us, against criminals, terrorists and tyrants.

Ms. Bovy is either a fool, or a NWO shill. I think she is the latter, but then again, she is a fool to believe in, and be a shill for the NWO.

It is for all rational people, who care about their families, to resist, at all cost, this attempt to disarm us. To be disarmed is to be subservient; a slave.
