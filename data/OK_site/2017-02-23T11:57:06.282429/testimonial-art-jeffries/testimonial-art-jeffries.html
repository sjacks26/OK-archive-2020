Testimonial – Art Jeffries

0 0 0 0
by Shorty Dawkins , January 23, 2015

Testimonial: My name is Art. I am 48 years old, married, and the father of 2 young children. We live in Des Moines Iowa. I joined the Army right out of high school back in 1984, and I am a veteran of Desert Storm. Like the rest of you I see what is going on here in our country and it is very upsetting to me. It seems like everybody lives in fear of something nowadays. Fear of terrorism, fear of the growing police state, fear of impending economic collapse, fear of Pandemics, fear of GMO foods, vaccines, I could go on and on. Fear has been rammed down our throats daily since Sept. 11th 2001. Many times I have heard people say that they fear what the future holds for their children. Well my brothers and sisters, I for one, am tired of living in fear! The time has come to take a stand and secure the future of this nation not only for us, but for future generations as well. It is with great pride that I join the ranks of Oath Keepers.
