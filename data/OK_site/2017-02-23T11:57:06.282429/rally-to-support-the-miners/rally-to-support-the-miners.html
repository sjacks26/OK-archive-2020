Rally To Support The Miners!

0 0 0 0
April 21, 2015
 –
The owners of the Sugar Pine Mine have called for a rally of support for not only themselves, but all miners. It is important that everyone who can show up for the rally does so, for the bigger the response, the better the likelihood of a peaceful, just, lawful solution to this matter. The BLM has a history of running roughshod over miners, ranchers and farmers Constitutional rights. It is time to stand up for ALL the rights of the miners, and others under the thumb of the BLM. Come to the rally. Show your support for these miners, and see they get their Constitutionally protected Rights secured. – Shorty Dawkins, Associate Editor
