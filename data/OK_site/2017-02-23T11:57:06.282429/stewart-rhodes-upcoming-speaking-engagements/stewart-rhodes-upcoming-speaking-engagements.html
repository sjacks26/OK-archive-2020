Friday Evening Program:
Our 2015 District Convention banquet speaker will be Stewart Rhodes from a group called Oath Keepers. Oath Keepers are a group of former military, police and firefighters who believe in keeping the oath of the Constitution. They call themselves the Guardians of the Republic. Some of the Oath Keepers were down in Ferguson, Missouri during the riots there last fall. It will be very interesting to hear what Stewart has to say about that. Watch Oath Keeper’s Video.
Friday night is Honor Veterans Night! We are using our banquet this year to honor all veterans in attendance. If you served in our armed forces in any capacity, your banquet ticket will only be the cost of the meal ($10). Any veterans coming just to listen will be admitted free.

SECOND AMENDMENT RALLY

Saturday, April 18 at 1:00pm – 4:00pm in CDT

Iowa State Capitol, in Des Moines, Iowa. West lower Terrace, across the street from the Capitol front stairs

On Saturday 18 April Iowa Oath Keepers,in conjunction with Tea Party

Oathkeepers in the surrounding states,and we hope to meet and get to
know many of them. Stewart Rhodes will be our featured speaker,and we
thank him for giving up his free day between two engagements in other
states to headline our event.

Chairman for the Americas Party, will give the invocation and a speech

has offered to provide his expertize,and top of the line equipment
,and also MC the event. This will be an open-carry rally This link
