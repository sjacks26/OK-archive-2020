May 3, 2015

The Sugar Pine Mine Operation is an ongoing operation.  Help is needed, as well as donations. Stewart Rhodes and Mary Emerick discuss the Operation, its scope and the need for everyone, not just local Patriots to stand up to the BLM’s running roughshod over these miners. The Operation’s purpose is to ensure the miners get their day in court. The Oath Keepers, and others, by standing with the miners, will see they get their due process rights. The BLM cannot be trusted to give them their due process rights. It is We The People, standing side by side with these miners, that will allow them their day in court.
