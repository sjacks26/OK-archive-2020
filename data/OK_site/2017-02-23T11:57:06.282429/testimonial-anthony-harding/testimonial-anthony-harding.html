Testimonial – Anthony Harding

0 0 0 0
February 18, 2015

Testimonial: I’ll make this simple. Since I was 5 years old, I swore an allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, every morning before class. When I turned 18, I joined the US Air Force and swore an oath to protect my country from all enemies, foreign and domestic. I have never sworn an allegiance to any political party or to a corporation or union at any time in my life. I have sworn a personal oath to protect the Constitution and the Bill of Rights. I have sworn an oath to protect my wife and my children. All of these sworn oaths I will give my life for and for the protection of this country, I would also give my life for.

If what I believe in, and say here to anyone who reads my words, puts me on some kind of watch list or database, so be it. I am no coward, I used my real name and I believe 100% in what I say here.

I am not afraid of my government. I am not afraid to face tyranny.

Anthony Harding
US Air Force Veteran
Commonwealth of Virginia
