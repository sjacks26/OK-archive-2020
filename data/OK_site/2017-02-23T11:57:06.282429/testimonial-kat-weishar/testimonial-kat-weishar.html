February 12, 2015

Testimonial: We also serve who only stand and wait…

I am not, nor have I been a member of the military, a LEO,  nor a Firefighter.

I am a citizen who stands in awe of all who have done so. I swore to uphold the same oath with the purpose of working to honor those who have given their lives and those who continue still protecting mine.

I AM AN OATHKEEPER AND THANK GOD THAT I CAN BE ! I will protect, defend and cherish our Constitution till I am no more.
