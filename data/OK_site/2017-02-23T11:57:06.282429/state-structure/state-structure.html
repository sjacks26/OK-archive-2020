STATE STRUCTURE:

Outline written by OK Lady

______________________________

 

Each State is registered within the state as a legal or chartered “chapter”
of the Nevada corporation, Oath Keepers.

So called ‘regional and/or County ‘chapters’ are not covered
under this provision.

The State leadership structure is not listed in the by-laws
and is much simpler:

Board Of Directors Vetted Positions: background check, etc.
President – prior service only – paying member
Acting President – Associate Member, no prior service – paying member
Vice President – all paying members

Board Of Directors approval w/ State Chapter President (SCP) referral: all members
Secretary
Treasurer
Sgt-at-arms
Moderators-Point Of Contact (POC)

Volunteer with SCP approval: all members
Regional Coordinator-Point of Contact (POC)
District Coordinator

Volunteers: all members
Local meeting coordinators

All members include ‘special guests’.
