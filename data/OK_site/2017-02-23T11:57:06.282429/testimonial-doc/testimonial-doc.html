February 17, 2015

Testimonial: I took my first oath to uphold the Constitution at the age of 18. This oath was taken after joining the U.S. Navy in 1969. I took my second oath at the age of 27, this was done as a Law Enforcement Officer. Both times I took the oath were forever planted in my heart. I take great pride in my country and it’s history. I firmly believe that GOD has chosen America to be the salvation of mankind. I will not sit and watch her destroyed. The many sacrifices made by my Brothers and Sisters will not fade from my memory. I stand firm as a Christian Soldier ready to serve at all cost. I go to my knees only to pray to my Lord God.
