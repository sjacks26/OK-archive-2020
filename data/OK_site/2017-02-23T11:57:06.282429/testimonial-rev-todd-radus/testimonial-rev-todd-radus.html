Testimonial – Rev. Todd Radus

0 0 0 0
January 30, 2015

Testimonial: Being a decendent of President John Adams and a true patriot, I intend on keeping my oath to the constitution of the United States, and to defend against foreign and domestic enemies. I am still willing and able to give my life for my fellow countrymen who support liberty and the freedoms granted to us by God, and laid out by our Forefathers through the Constitution and Bill Of Rights.
