Clarksville (TN) Recruiting Center Offers A Thank You

0 0 0 0
July 20, 2015

Guarding Recruitment Centers in NH

Stewart Rhodes:  “Within hours of the attack on the recruiting station and reserve center in Chattanooga, Tennessee, the Tennessee Oath Keepers were on post, guarding recruitment centers across the state. Here is a thank you email they got from a Clarksville, TN recruiter.”

The letter of thanks:

Classification: UNCLASSIFIED
Caveats: FOUO

To all concerned,

We have two young men here at our office today. They opened by stating their purpose for being here is to provide the center with security. As a recruiter I appreciate that very much. Thank you for assisting us in this ever changing nation. While I did not get their names I will do that later. Just letting you know that we, the US Army, appreciate your caring and concern for our well-being.

Respectfully,
Scott A. Byrd
SSG, USA
U.S. Army Career Center
Clarksville, Tn 37040
(o)931-647-1708
(c)931-334-9660

Oath Keepers are guarding recruiting centers across the country, in Tennessee, Arkansas, South Carolina, Ohio, New Hamshire, among other states, as part of our “Operation Protect the Protectors.” We will be issuing a formal national call to action on that project later today. Stay tuned.
