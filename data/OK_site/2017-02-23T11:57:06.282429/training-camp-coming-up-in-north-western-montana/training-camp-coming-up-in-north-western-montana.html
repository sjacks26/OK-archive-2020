Upcoming Tactical Training Camp in North Western Montana

0 0 0 0
by Jason Van Tatenhove , February 10, 2016

On the upcoming weekend of Friday, February 19th through Sunday February 21st Members of the Northern Idaho Training Center (NITC) will be hosting in Marion Montana an Intermediate level training camp.  The focus of this camp will be patrolling, ambushes, defensive positions, etc. There are no prerequisites to this training anyone is welcome as long as you are able to furnish the mandatory equipment and can pay the entry fee. All participants need to meet at the rally point at McGregor Lake Lodge (in the parking lot), 12250 U.S. Hwy 2 W, Marion, MT 59925 at the designated times.

 

On a personal note I would like to mention that I am often asked which trainings I would recommend for people in the American Redoubt Region. I can not more strongly recommend the instructors running the Northern Idaho Training Center. There is no one out there that offers the level of in-depth training for the same values.

~Jason Van Tatenhove

 

If Interested contact Jason Van Tatenhove for more information: Jason@oathkeepers.org
