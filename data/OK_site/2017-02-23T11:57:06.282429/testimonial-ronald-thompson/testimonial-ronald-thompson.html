Testimonial – Ronald Thompson

0 0 0 0
February 13, 2015

Testimonial:  I am a patriot. My GGG grandfather fought for freedom under General Francis Marion (The Swamp Fox) My GGG Grandfather survived that ordeal and went on to fight in the Indian Wars in the state of Mississippi. My family, on both maternal and paternal sides are very much a patriot group, going back to the 1500’s in this country. We have been involved since before this country was occupied by white men. My GGG grandfather was a Cherokee Indian and still he fought for the freedom of this country.

With this kind of ancestral background how could I be anything less than a PATRIOT and stand up to the forces that want to take my rights as a freeman from me.
