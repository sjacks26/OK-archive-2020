Testimonial:  I first took my oath in 1995. Since then I have taken the oath 3 more times. Every time that I take the oath it gives me chills to know that I am
reaffirming something so much bigger than my self. It wasn’t until recently that I started to notice that our freedoms were being taken from our great nation under the guise of security.

It saddens me to say that so many of us have been asleep for much to long, and just blindly followed the direction of our elected officials. I am wide awake now and I try to encourage the Airman under me to learn and understand what there oath really means.

I am continuing to learn at a rapid pace and soak up all the information that I can.

I, Steven __________, do solemnly swear (or affirm) that I will support and defend
the Constitution of the United States against all enemies, foreign and domestic;
that I will bear true faith and allegiance to the same; and that I will obey the
orders of the President of the United States and the orders of the officers
appointed over me, according to regulations and the Uniform Code of Military
Justice. So help me God.
