Testimonial – Woody Page

0 0 0 0
February 24, 2015

Sgt. Page 7 Medals, WWII Hero

 

Testimonial: This great country of ours has a fine past. The founders were bold leaders who had a vision of Liberty and Freedom. Our Constitution is something worth supporting.  Past, present and future our banner and Constitution stand for what I believe in.

My prayer is for our respect for it and those who have died to keep it for us. God bless America. In God we trust…
