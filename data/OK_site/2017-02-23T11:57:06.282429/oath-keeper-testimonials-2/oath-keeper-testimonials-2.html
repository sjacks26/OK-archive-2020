Jim:

Testimonial: Greetings my brothers and sisters…

Just recently joined Oath Keepers even though I have known about the organization for a while. I am a military retiree who served this great nation faithfully and honorably for over 22 years.

I did 4 years in the Marine Corps and attained the rank of Sergeant. After a seven year break in service, I joined the Navy as a Hospital Corpsman. I did 18+ years in the Navy and retired as a Chief Independent Duty Hospital Corpsman.

Unfortunately, growing up I had little interest in politics and didn’t really follow the agendas of the parties or those in office. It has only been less than 10 years since my awakening to the harsh realities and wrong doings of our government.

It is quite clear, at least in my eyes, that the current administration does not have the interest of the people who put them in office in mind. The Constitution has been shoved aside and abandoned as those in office try to force their own agendas without abiding by the Law of the Land. This my friends is Tyranny.

From my very first day in the military, as all of you know, I took an oath. The key phrase of that oath says that I will “protect and serve the Constitution of the United States of America against all enemies, foreign and domestic”. ALL enemies…foreign and DOMESTIC! And I don’t know about all of you, but my Oath of Enlistment did not come with an expiration date.

Until now I have only defended our rights by writing our elected officials. I have written everyone from my local Congressman, to my State Senators, clear up to the President of the United States (and I use this title loosely) himself. I’ve found that as an individual, words whether voiced or written, seem to fall on deaf ears.

Now as a member of Oath Keepers, I hope to have more influence trying to enlighten fellow Americans who still follow as mindless rodents being lured by the “Pied Piper” to their proverbial deaths. And maybe…we can show those we have put in office that it is the voice of the people they should be following and not their own agendas. There is strength in numbers!

Thank you for listening to my rant, and thank you for having me as a member…
