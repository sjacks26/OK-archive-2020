by Shorty Dawkins , January 26, 2015

Testimonial: I joined the US Army on Aug 31, 1962 to Aug 30, 1965.
When sworn in that day I took the Oath to defend the Constitution, my country and family with a deep sense of pride and a sincere heart felt conviction.  That Oath is just as important today as it was then, and I will proudly stand next to my fellows to defend this country with my life, my home and fortune. I will not comply with a law that is unconstitutional period. I will be 72 in March of this year 2015.
God Bless America!
