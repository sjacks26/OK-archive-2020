Testimonial – James Huelle

0 0 0 0
February 13, 2015

Testimonial: “…Cities may be rebuilt, and a People reduced to Poverty, may acquire fresh Property: But a Constitution of Government once changed from Freedom, can never be restored. Liberty once lost is lost forever. When the People once surrendered their share in the Legislature, and their Right of defending the Limitations upon the Government, and of resisting every Encroachment upon them, they can never regain it.”  ― John Adams

We cannot allow Liberty to be lost! Being in the military for over 15 yrs and a public servant for 16, I hope that I will never waiver from “serving the people” in THEIR best interest AND also stay the course in my belief and protection of our Constitution. For those who beleive the Constitution and Bill of Rights are just “old Parchment” which don’t apply anymore in our society, I feel pity, but make no mistake I will not feel sympathy when it is time to protect the Constitution to which I have taken an Oath to uphold.
