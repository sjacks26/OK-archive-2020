Guardians Of The Republic To Speak At Tea Party Event

0 0 0 0
April 2, 2015

This article comes from the Long Prairie Leader.

Oath Keepers are a group of former military, police, and firefighters who believe in keeping the oath of the Constitution. They call themselves the Guardians of the Republic. Some of the Oath Keepers were down in Ferguson, MO, during the riots. Where will they be next?

Monday, April 13, 2015, Stewart Rhodes, founder of Oath Keepers, will be speaking at the Browerville Community Center. Program starts at 6:30 p.m. Doors to be open earlier.

Learn concrete steps for the average citizen to do in order to save the Republic. Honoring the oath, restoring patriotism!

All veterans, government officials, law enforcement, school board and officials, and those that value our Constitution are welcome.

Refreshments will be served. Free will offering to cover expenses would be appreciated.

Sponsored by Central Minnesota Tea Party Patriots.
