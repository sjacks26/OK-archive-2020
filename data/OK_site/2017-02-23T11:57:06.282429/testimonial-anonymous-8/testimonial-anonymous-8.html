February 26, 2015

Testimonial: I have not been a part of the Active Military, but I do live in a military community. I believe it is every adult citizen’s RESPONSIBILITY to bear arms and provide for the civil defense, regardless of being part of “this man’s army.” I have encouraged family, friends and neighbors to get licensed, train at ranges and practice on a regular basis to do their part to protect our little part of the land of the free. I currently serve as Chair for the Libertarian Party in my County and am a Senior Member of Young Americans for Liberty (YAL), and actively befriend those that are willing to keep an oath to protect the citizens, freedoms, and way of life of this country. God Bless America, God Bless Oath Keepers.
